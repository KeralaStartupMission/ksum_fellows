Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) have been actively initiating various programmes for developing the student entrepreneurship in the state. About Fellowship

Government of Kerala in its attempt to create a congenial startup ecosystem in Kerala, is offering a one-year fellowship to young graduates interested in working with technology startups and student entrepreneurs. This is a flagship programme of Government of Kerala towards promotion of innovation and entrepreneurship across the State. Kerala Startup Mission is the implementing agency for the same. According to this Kerala Startup Mission has selected 15 young graduates as fellows in its first batch, who will be a campaigner for technology startup activities in the state and will conduct various events like entrepreneurship awareness camps, maker sessions, hackathons and ideathons in colleges and other institutions in Kerala.

The fellowship is offered for a period of 12 months in two batches comprising of 15 members each. The applications for the fellowship are called in the months of May and November and fellowship period will begin from the month of June and December respectively.

Technology and Innovation Fellowship

Activities


  • To evangelize the technology startup activities in the state under KSUM
  • To conduct various entrepreneurship and innovation related activities like hackathons, workshops, seminars, maker sessions etc across Kerala
  • The fellows will have to complete goals assigned to him/her on a timely basis.
  • This fellowship should not be considered as a permanent employment with Kerala Startup Mission and will be valid for a maximum period of 1 year only.

Conditions

  • The Kerala Startup Mission will not accept hard-copy applications.
  • There will be NO fees charged for filling and submitting the application form.
  • Applications are non-binding.
  • Only students who have completed the degree course need to apply

What we’re looking for ?
We’re looking for people who are curious, bring diverse backgrounds and perspectives, can communicate well with technical and non-technical people, and are passionate creating a startup ecosystem.
